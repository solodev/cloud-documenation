## Services

### Current Services

Existing services are shown as cards on the dashboard. Each card displays a thumbnail of the service, its name, by whom it was launched and is clickable to <a href="/services/read/">access the service</a> directly.

><img src="../../../images/dashboard8.jpg" alt="dashboard8" style="width: 100%; display: block"></a>

### Services Settings

Click on the gear wheel icon on a service card to open <a href="/services/settings/">service settings</a>.

><img src="../../../images/dashboard10.jpg" alt="dashboard10" style="width: 10%; display: block"></a>

### Add Service

Click on the "Add Service" card to <a href="/services/add/">add a new service</a>.
