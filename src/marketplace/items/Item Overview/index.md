## Item Management

Manage your item features.

<img src="../../../../../images/itemmanagement.jpg" alt="itemmanagement" style="width: 50%; display: block"></a>

**Name** | **Description** 
:--- | ---
SKU | Enter the SKU.
Title | Enter the item title.
Publisher | Enter the Publisher.
Publisher/App URL| Enter the Publisher/App URL.
Product Icon | Place the product icon by using the button **Browse**. It appears on the store page or in the detail header. <p>**Dimensions: 90px by 90px. File format: JPEG, PNG, or SVG.**
Logo Icon Alt | Enter the logo icon alt.
Product Logo | Place the product logo by using the button **Browse**. It is for use in other store applications. <p>**File format: JPEG, PNG, or SVG.**
Logo Alt | Enter the logo alt.
Product Image | Place the product image. It shows on the store page and replaces the product logo and icon. <p>**Used for full images instead of icons or logos, like modules. Width: 300px.**
Product Image Alt | Enter the product image alt.
Featured Image | Place featured image. It shows in detail page. <p>**Width: 600px.**
Featured Image Alt | Enter the featured image alt.
Description / Intro | Enter the description/intro. It shows on the store page. <p>**Character count: Up to 90.**
Product Overview | Enter the product overview. It shows in detail page. <p>**(Required - Don't use html tags, just text). Character count: up to 200.**
Product Features | Enter the product features. It shows in detail page.
Product Details | Enter the product details. It shows in the right column of the detail page.
Product support text | Enter the product support text. It shows in detail page.
Product Instructions | Enter the product instructions. It shows in detail page.
Product Documentation | Enter the product documentation.

## Status

Manage the item status.

<img src="../../../../../images/itemstatus.jpg" alt="itemstatus" style="width: 50%; display: block"></a>

**Name** | **Description** 
:--- | ---
Choose Status | Choose the item status.
Choose Start Date | Choose the start date.
Choose End Date |  Choose the end date.

!!!Note:
You can choose from three status types:

- **Publish**
- **Preview**
- **Draft**
!!!
