# Delete Organization

Click the orange "Delete" button to delete your organization.

><img src="../../../images/org-delete.jpg" alt="org-delete" style="width: 60%; display: block"></a>

Type DELETE in all-caps in the text field and click the red "Delete" button to delete your organization.

><img src="../../../images/org-delete2.jpg" alt="org-delete2" style="width: 100%; display: block"></a>

!!! Alert:
Deleting an organization will permanently remove the organization and all of its content and cannot be undone.
!!!
