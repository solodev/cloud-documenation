# Add Organization

Adding an organization to your org in Solodev Cloud is a simple process, and can be completed in minutes by following the steps below.

## Step 1: Add Organization

To add an organization you need to provide a name for your organization and a billing email.

><img src="../../../images/org-add.jpg" alt="org-add" style="width: 100%; display: block"></a>

### Organization Name

Enter your company name.

><img src="../../../images/org-add2.jpg" alt="org-add2" style="width: 50%; display: block"></a>

### Billing Email

Enter a valid email address.

><img src="../../../images/org-add3.jpg" alt="org-add3" style="width: 50%; display: block"></a>

!!! Please Note:
This field currently cannot be changed here. The billing email is an email of your user account. You can change the billing email in <a href="/organizations/rename/">profile page</a>.
!!!

### Add

Once you’ve completed all of the fields, click the blue “Add” button and your organization will be created.

><img src="../../../images/page-add3.jpg" alt="page-add3" style="width: 40%; display: block"></a>

!!! Please Note:
The organization build process may take several minutes to complete after clicking the "Add" button. Do not close or quit your browser during this process.
!!!
