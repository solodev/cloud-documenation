# Add Service

Adding a service in Solodev Cloud is a simple process, and can be completed in minutes by following the steps below.

---

## Step 1: Pick a service

You can choose between variety of different services deviced into three groups: Cloud, Marketing and Metaverse.

><img src="../../../images/services-add-service.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 70%; display: block"></a>

Use the lef hand side menu to swich between the categories.

><img src="../../../images/services-add-leftmenu.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 10%; display: block"></a>

Once you have choosen the service of your interest, click 'BUY NOW' button on the bottom of the banner.

><img src="../../../images/services-buynow.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 15%; display: block"></a>

## Step 2: Finilize your purchase

You will be taken to a service info page where you will be able to set up plan that will fit your needs.

><img src="../../../images/services-setplan.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 70%; display: block"></a>

After making your choice simply click 'Buy Now' button on the bottom of the page.

><img src="../../../images/services-buynowfinal.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 15%; display: block"></a>

You will be taken to the 'Review Order' page where if needed you will have an option to add add-ons.

><img src="../../../images/services-addons.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 70%; display: block"></a>

After chosing what you need simply click 'Complete Purchase' button.

><img src="../../../images/services-completepurchase.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 15%; display: block"></a>

!!!
***Please Note** : In order to finilize your order you will need to set up your [billing and payment details](../../settings/billing/index.md)*. 
!!!

When successful you will be presented with your receipt. From this page you will be also able to install your service on Solodev Cloud. 

><img src="../../../images/services-thankyou.jpg" alt="services details" style="width: 70%; display: block"></a>
