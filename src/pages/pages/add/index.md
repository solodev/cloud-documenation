# Add Page

Adding a page to your org in Solodev Cloud is a simple process, and can be completed in minutes by following the steps below.

## Step 1: Add Page

To add page you need to provide a title for your page and choose site.

><img src="../../../images/pages-add4.jpg" alt="pages-add4" style="width: 70%; display: block"></a>

### Title

The title that you provide will be displayed in the front-end.

><img src="../../../images/add-title.jpg" alt="add-title" style="width: 50%; display: block"></a>

### Site

Select your site form the dropdown list. You can also <a href="/pages/sites/add/">add a new site</a>.

><img src="../../../images/site.jpg" alt="site" style="width: 50%; display: block"></a>

><img src="../../../images/site-add.jpg" alt="site-add" style="width: 50%; display: block"></a>

### Add

Once you’ve completed all of the fields, click the blue “Add” button and your site will be created.

><img src="../../../images/sites-add-site-addbtn.jpg" alt="click add button" style="width: 70%; display: block"></a>

!!! Please Note:
The page build process may take several minutes to complete after clicking the "Add" button. Do not close or quit your browser duing this process.
!!!
